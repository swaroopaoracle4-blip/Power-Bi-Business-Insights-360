##  Project Overview
**Business Insights 360** is an end-to-end **Power BI analytics solution** developed for *AtliQ Technologies*, a global consumer electronics company operating across **27 markets** and serving **75 customers**.

The project transforms raw business data into **actionable insights** across:
- Finance
- Sales
- Marketing
- Supply Chain
- Executive Management  

The solution enables leadership to move toward **data-driven decision-making**.

---

##  Business Challenges
- AtliQ became one of the **fastest-growing electronics companies**, expanding rapidly into new regions.
- The company faced **significant losses in Latin America** after opening its own stores.
- Business decisions were largely based on **surveys and intuition**, rather than data.
- Existing **Excel-based reporting** was no longer scalable for a growing organization.
- Leadership identified analytics adoption as a **top strategic priority**.

---

##  Project Objectives
- Analyze AtliQ‚Äôs overall business performance using data.
- Identify **key insights and trends** to support better decision-making.
- Enable a transition from **intuition-based** to **data-driven** strategies.
- Support sustainable business growth through **clear, interactive dashboards**.

##  Key Dashboard Insights

###  Finance View
Provides a comprehensive overview of **financial performance** through detailed **Profit & Loss analysis** across **markets, customers, and products**.

####  Key Features
-  **Core KPIs**: Net Sales, Gross Margin %, and Net Profit % tracking  
-  **Year-over-Year (YoY) comparison** and performance vs targets  
-  **Monthly sales trend analysis** to identify growth patterns  
-  **Detailed P&L statement** with revenue, cost, and margin breakdown  
-  **Top & Bottom customer and product analysis** to identify performance drivers  

---
###  Sales View
The **Sales View** provides insights into **Net Sales, Gross Margin (GM), and Gross Margin % (GM%)**, with detailed breakdowns by **customer and product segments**.  
Interactive tooltips enable users to explore **monthly sales trends**, helping sales teams monitor performance over time.

####  Key Features
-  **Customer & product-level performance analysis** (Net Sales, GM$, GM%)  
-  **Performance matrix** plotting GM% vs Net Sales based on target gap tolerance  
-  **Unit economics analysis** with detailed COGS breakdown  
-  **Regional sales performance comparison**  
-  **Time-based sales trend analysis** for growth tracking  

---

###  Marketing View
The **Marketing View** analyzes the impact of **operational costs, advertising & promotional spend**, and **regional profitability variations** to evaluate marketing effectiveness.

####  Key Features
-  **Product-level performance analysis** using Net Sales, GM%, and Net Profit %  
-  **Region, market, and customer performance comparison**  
-  **Performance matrix** based on GM% and Net Profit % to assess marketing efficiency  
-  **Market positioning insights** to support strategic planning  
-  **Overall marketing cost tracking** and spend effectiveness  

---

###  Supply Chain View
The **Supply Chain View** focuses on evaluating **forecast accuracy, demand variance, and inventory risk** to help proactively manage **excess inventory and out-of-stock situations**.

####  Key Features
-  **Forecast accuracy tracking** with trend analysis  
-  **Net Error and Absolute Error (ABS Error)** monitoring for demand planning improvement  
-  **Customer- and product-level forecast performance analysis**  
-  **Inventory risk assessment** with excess stock and stock-out indicators  
-  **Supply chain KPI trend analysis** over time  

---

###  Executive View
The **Executive View** provides leadership with a **holistic snapshot of company performance**, highlighting overall financial health, key risks, and growth opportunities.  
While AtliQ‚Äôs sales continue to grow, the analysis emphasizes the need to **optimize costs, balance inventory, and improve forecast accuracy** to sustain long-term profitability.

####  Key Features
-  **Overall Performance (2022 EST)**  
  - Net Sales: **3.74B**, reflecting strong year-over-year growth  
  - Gross Margin: **38.1%**, indicating stable and healthy profitability  

-  **Regional Risk Insights**  
  - **North America and Latin America** show signs of **excess inventory**, suggesting over-forecasting or demand planning gaps  

-  **Top 5 Customers & Top 5 Products**  
  - Quick visibility into primary revenue contributors  

-  **Revenue Contribution by Product Group (2022 EST)**  
  - **PC Group** as the highest revenue driver, followed by **Peripherals, Accessories, and Storage**  

-  **Market Share Analysis**  
  - Competitive comparison of **AtliQ vs Dell, Lenovo, and HP** to assess market positioning
